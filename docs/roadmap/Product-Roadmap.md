# Product Roadmap

## ERP Intelligence Platform

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Owner:** Helder Gonçalves

---

# 1. Purpose

The Product Roadmap defines the long-term evolution of the ERP Intelligence Platform.

It provides a strategic view of how the product will evolve from its first Minimum Viable Product (MVP) into a complete enterprise-grade ERP platform.

This roadmap focuses exclusively on product evolution.

Technical implementation details are documented elsewhere.

---

# 2. Product Vision

The ERP Intelligence Platform aims to become a modern, modular and AI-powered ERP solution capable of supporting small and medium-sized enterprises while demonstrating best practices in enterprise software engineering.

Every release should deliver measurable business value.

---

# 3. Product Principles

The product roadmap follows these principles:

* Business value first.
* Build small, improve continuously.
* Deliver working software frequently.
* AI is an enhancement, not a replacement.
* Quality over quantity.
* Modular evolution.
* Simplicity before complexity.

---

# 4. Release Strategy

The product will evolve through incremental releases.

Each release must produce a usable application.

# 6. Success Metrics

The roadmap will be considered successful when:

* Every release delivers business value.
* Releases remain independent.
* Product quality continuously improves.
* Documentation evolves with the product.
* Technical debt remains under control.

---

# 7. Relationship with Other Documents

This roadmap should be read together with:

* Project Charter
* Product Requirements Document (PRD)
* Software Architecture Document (SAD)
* Engineering Handbook
* Learning Roadmap
* Product Backlog

The Product Roadmap defines **what** will be delivered.

The Product Backlog defines **how the work is organised**.

Sprint documents define **when each feature is implemented**.

---

# 8. Roadmap Governance

The roadmap is a living document.

It shall be reviewed at the end of every major release.

Changes should only be introduced when they provide clear business or technical value.

Continuous evolution is one of the core principles of the ERP Intelligence Platform.
