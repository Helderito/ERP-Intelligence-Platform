# Project Charter

## ERP Intelligence Platform

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Date:** July 2026  
**Owner:** Helder Gonçalves

---

# 1. Introduction

ERP Intelligence Platform is a personal and portfolio project designed to develop a modern, scalable and intelligent ERP platform, inspired by the best practices of enterprise ERP systems such as Primavera, SAP and Microsoft Dynamics.

Beyond building an application, this project is intended as a structured learning journey in Modern Software Engineering, Software Architecture, Artificial Intelligence, DevOps and Business Intelligence.

The project will be developed as if it were a real commercial product, following professional software management methodologies and relying on Artificial Intelligence agents to support the development process.

---

# 2. Product Vision

Develop a modern ERP platform based on a cloud-native architecture, prepared for integration with Artificial Intelligence, enabling organisations to manage business processes efficiently and intelligently.

The platform shall serve as a practical demonstration of competencies in:

- Software Engineering
- Software Architecture
- Artificial Intelligence
- Full Stack Development
- DevOps
- Data Analytics
- Business Intelligence

---

# 3. Mission

Build a modern ERP solution using industry best practices, using Artificial Intelligence tools as development accelerators, without compromising the quality of the architecture and the code.

# 11. Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful when:

- The architecture is documented.
- All core modules are implemented.
- The system has automated tests.
- Deployment is fully automated.
- Documentation is complete.
- The platform has Artificial Intelligence integration.
- Analytical dashboards exist.
- The project can be presented as a professional portfolio.

---

# 12. Long-Term Vision

This project shall evolve continuously, keeping pace with the evolution of software development and Artificial Intelligence technologies.

More than an application, the goal is to build a reference platform for experimentation, continuous learning and demonstration of technical competencies.

The ERP Intelligence Platform will be the author's primary portfolio project, reflecting his evolution as a Software Engineer, Data Specialist and Artificial Intelligence professional applied to software development.
